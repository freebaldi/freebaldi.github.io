🏫 SCHOOL FEATURES
Doors: Regular doors (brown) block your path — press E or click to open. They auto-close after a few seconds. Double doors (yellow) are always open.
Lockers: 🔴 Red = decorative. 🔵 Blue = hide inside (press E) to escape enemies. 🟢 Green = store up to 2 items (press E).
Exits: 4 exits on floor 1. Once you collect all 7 notebooks, exits start sealing until only 1 remains — find it fast!
Classrooms: Rooms with doors that you can open. Notebooks are inside — but so are some characters!
Cafeteria: Large room in the south — Gotta Sweep patrols here. Sometimes has items on tables.
YTP (You Thought Points): Earned by collecting notebooks (+10 each). Spent at Jonathan's store for items.
Endless Mode: No exits, random events, survive as long as possible for high score.
NULL Mode: Unlocked after escaping Story Mode. The school is wrong. Something is watching. Collect 7 notebooks and face IT to escape.
